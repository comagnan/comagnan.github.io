title:  "This cursed box"
date:   2026-04-28 10:00:00 -0400

---

This cursed box, they say,<br>
was invented,<br>
not found.<br>
<br>
Those who use it don't go mad,<br>
not exactly,<br>
just something like it.<br>
<br>
It's a window into parallel worlds;<br>
all of them, in fact,<br>
there aren't that many.<br>
<br>
And witnessing possibility,<br>
in itself,<br>
is not a problem.<br>
<br>
But inevitably,<br>
one seeks the familiar,<br>
the perspective of the scattered selves.<br>
<br>
So one experiences the success,<br>
the fulfilment,<br>
on the paths they didn't take.<br>
<br>
And that knowledge,<br>
once obtained,<br>
never fades.<br>
<br>
Ah, prideful beings we are,<br>
to so strongly long to have been,<br>
the best version of ourselves.<br>
